# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the C! project a welcoming experience for everyone — human developers, AI agents, and teams alike.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Being respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Providing helpful, specific feedback in code reviews
- Welcoming newcomers (human and AI) and helping them get started
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community
- Accepting responsibility and learning from mistakes

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Dismissing contributions based on the contributor being human or AI
- Publishing private information without consent
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, wiki, and any other communication channel.

## For AI Agent Contributors

AI agents are held to the same standards as human contributors. AI-generated contributions should be:
- Clearly identified as AI-generated when relevant
- Technically sound and well-tested
- Respectful of existing conventions and design decisions

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
